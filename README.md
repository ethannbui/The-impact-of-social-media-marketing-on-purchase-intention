# Social Media Marketing & Purchase Intention

## Dissertation – Blueseed Digital Case Study

This qualitative dissertation investigates the impact of social media marketing on purchase intention in Vietnam, using Blueseed Digital Company as a case study.

Through semi-structured interviews (6 participants) and thematic analysis, the study identified four key factors influencing purchase intention:
- Content of the advertisement (meaningful message, entertainment, timing)
- Information provided in the advertisement
- Electronic word-of-mouth (reviews & social proof)
- Interaction between brand and customers

The findings show that social media marketing significantly influences purchase intention, with **brand–customer interaction and electronic word-of-mouth** being the strongest drivers.


## Methodology
- Research Philosophy: Interpretivism  
- Approach: Inductive  
- Strategy: Case Study  
- Method: Qualitative (Semi-structured interviews)  
- Analysis: Thematic Analysis
